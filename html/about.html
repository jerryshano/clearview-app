About Clearview Carehome

Our facility is located in a relaxing country setting in the hills of Hollister. Clearview Carehome has been happily, family owned and operated since 1995. We specialize in the care of the elderly and can accept memory care or assisted living residents.

Clearview Carehome takes the time to build a care plan unique to each individual resident. Care plan factors include: physical, functional and emotional needs. Developing a thorough care plan provides our staff structure to better assist residents in their everyday needs.

Feel free to come and visit us. We would be happy to show you our picturesque facility. Schedule a tour with us to see more.
