Welcome to Clearview Carehome
Assisted Living

Clearview Carehome is an assisted living and memory care facility for the elderly. We generally serve seniors from 80 years old and up. Whether your loved one has alzheimer's, or is just in need of some assistance, we have just the place for you. Our staff go above and beyond in caring for the everyday needs of our residents.

The staff cater to the individual needs of our residents. Our home cooked meals are based on our residents choice. The nurse knows the appropriate nutritional needs for those residents who are on special diet such as diabetes, high blood pressure medications and many other dietary requirements.

Clearview Carehome is owned and operated by a Registered Nurse. The nurse has over 30 years of experience in the care of the elderly and has wound care expertise. Our staff extends a high level of care that makes us wholeheartedly unique.
